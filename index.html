Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, popularly known as the "Missile Man of India", was an eminent scientist and the 11th President of India. He is remembered for his emphasis on education, innovation, and nation-building.

Timeline & Achievements

1931: Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
1954: Graduated in Physics from St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirapalli.
1960: Joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
1980: Played a major role in India's first successful satellite launch.
1992: Appointed as Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister.
2002-2007: Served as the 11th President of India.
2015: Passed away while delivering a lecture in Shillong, inspiring millions till his last breath.
